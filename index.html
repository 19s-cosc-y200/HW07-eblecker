What is RentADog?

RentADog is a service where you can temporarily adopt a canine companion for up to 24 hours! This is perfect for:

  • Those who are considering adopting their own pup and want to get an idea of what this may entail
  • Students who are missing their own dogs from home
  • Tennants of an appartment who love dogs but are not allowed to have their own in their appartment
  • And anyone else who is interested in caring for a pup short-term!
